📖 How It Works

1
📷 Camera detects plate at barrier
2
🔍 System looks up plate in vehicle database
3
🔐 Checks active permission for this vehicle & barrier
4
⏰ Validates date range and time window
5
🚦 Barrier opens (GRANTED) or stays closed (DENIED)
6
📋 Event logged with datetime and result
